Installation:

=========================

Copy both files:

"MT-PowerDrumKit.dll"

AND (!)
 
"MT-PowerDrumKit-Content.PDK"

into the VST Plugins folder of your DAW.

Example:
C:\Program Files\Steinberg\VSTPlugins\

(Check the preferences of your DAW for your actual VST Plugins folder).

=========================

Doesn't work? Check out:
https://www.powerdrumkit.com/help.php
